• Tap Here to Install The Science Game 2.4.2 for IOS

Instructions

  1. Installation: Click on the above link on your IPad to install. This takes 2-10 minutes depending on network speed

  2. Registration: Once installed, touch to start the Science Game app. It will take you to a registration page. Fill details on this page and submit. The school id for CISB is already filled in.

  3. Email Confirmation: An email will come to the email address specified during registration. Click on the URL in the email to confirm your ownership of the email address

  4. Restart App: Close the Science Game app on the IPad and restart the app. Once the app syncs to the server (about a minute), all topics in Electromagnetism will be enabled.

  5. Note: The topics will remain enabled only for the duration of the promotion

  6. Reports:Activity of each user can be seen in the activity reports. The link for reports is sent separately.

  7. Need help?: Send email to info@mazalearn.com


How to Close IPad app: Double Touch home button to see list of active apps. Drag Science Game app towards the top of the screen and release.
