Science Game – Electromagnetism: Unleash the Einstein within you

July 5 2015 - As America celebrates its 239th Independence Day, Science Game is celebrating its 100,000th installation. Science Game – Electromagnetism enables you to learn science in your own way, at your own pace, in your own time.

Developed by Maza Learn a company whose aim is to make learning a fun-filled exploration, Science Game – Electromagnetism enables you to learn by doing, experimenting, making mistakes and learning from those mistakes.

With books and videos, the focus in on “teaching”. Science Game – Electromagnetism focusses on “learning by doing”, transforming you into an electromagnetism Guru in a few hours as you interact with fun-filled activities. A touchscreen phone or tablet is all you need to embark on your journey to become a scientist by exploring the fine details of the subject through intriguing 2D and 3D models.

Over 200 concepts in magnets, coils, dynamos and motors are explored in fun simulations, challenges and games with an intuitive interface. Molecules, electric currents and fields come to life on your screen, creating a clear picture in your head of exactly how electromagnetism works. A comprehensive quiz drills down on concepts, providing support for 9th and 10th grade students learning the CBSE, IGCSE and NGSS syllabuses. Educational App Store rates it at 4-stars and says “...this is a good quality educational app that offers a great deal for both the learner and the teacher"

Sridhar Sundaram, co-founder of Maza Learn, says: “In today's world, learning is not about ‘finding’ but about ‘minding’. Books and videos have a 20% retention rate - what drives real engagement and real learning is interactivity which has an astounding 90% retention.”

Science Game – Electromagnetism  is available for free from the Apple iTunes Store and Android Play Store and is compatible with all devices running iOS 5.1.1 or Android 3.0 or later.

Download Science Game – Electromagnetism today to see why science has never before been so accessible and so much fun.
