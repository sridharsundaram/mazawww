LCM is the Least Common Multiple and HCF is the Highest Common Factor.

Many children find these two concepts confusing and are not sure when to use what in real life and test problems.

Play this game and develop your intution about LCM and HCF.

Maza Learn is experimenting with fun cognitive learning models with a set of scenarios in which language or any other subject learning can be grounded in use.

The scenario engine allows a declarative specification of scenarios covering a use-case and corresponding adaptive game-based associations to learn from that scenario.
