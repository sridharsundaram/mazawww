Monkey Math is a series of games to make math intuitive.

LCM is the Least Common Multiple and HCF is the Highest Common Factor.

Many children find these two concepts confusing and are not sure when to use what in real life and test problems.

This game develops intution about LCM and HCF.

We want to make learning fun and accessible.

Paraphrasing Confucius -

I hear, I forget.
I see, I remember.
I do, I understand

We create fun games that provide intuition into concepts.
