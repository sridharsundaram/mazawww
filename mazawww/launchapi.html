Launch API

Overview

Any app on Android or on IOS can launch a specific instructional unit within Science Game - Electromagnetism by using a specific URL to invoke the app from within another app.

Example

The custom URL  is composed of a base part, a board part, a topic part, a subtopic part and a tutor part. For example:
mazalearn://www.mazalearn.com/science/igcse/electromagnetism?level=BarMagnetInduction&tutor=C1.5

Launch URL Details

The generic form of the custom URL is
mazalearn://www.mazalearn.com/science/board/topic?level=subtopic&tutor=tutorid
Here,
  • board can be one of
  • topic can be electromagnetism.  New topic soon to be made available is waves.
  • subtopic can be one of the subtopics of the topic. If topic is electromagnetism, it can be one of
  • tutorid corresponds to one of the tutors within the subtopic. It may also be tidbit to launch the Maza News with tidbit shown.
Further, the level and tutor parts of the URL are optional.
